About Us

Toter® started in the 1960s with an automated cart system for curbside garbage collection and has since become a leading manufacturer of two-wheeled carts for waste, recycling, and organics collection. Toter produces a variety of durable products, including caster carts, organics carts, stationary containers, and more. In 2007, Toter joined Wastequip, a top North American manufacturer of waste handling and recycling equipment.
